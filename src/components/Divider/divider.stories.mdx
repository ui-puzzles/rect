## Divider

Divider the difficent content area and sections.

## Examples

### default & align

The default is a horizontal dividing line. You also can contain a text when the mode is setted 'horizontal'.

If the Divider contains a text, you can specify the position of the texts by align.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="align">
    <h4 style={{ textAlign: 'center'}}>The Lost Love</h4>
    <Divider>William Wordsworth</Divider>
    <p>She dwelt among the untrodden ways beside the springs of dove.</p>
    <Divider align="left">align left</Divider>
    <p>A maid whom there were none to praise, and very few to love.</p>
    <Divider dashed />
    <p>A violet by a mossy stone, half hidden from the eye!</p>
    <Divider align="right">align right</Divider>
    <p>Fair as a star, when only one is shining in the sky! She lived unknown, and few could know, when lucy ceased to be.</p>
    <p>But she is in her grave, and oh, the difference to me!</p>
    <Divider />
  </Story>
</Canvas>

### mode

Specify mode as "vertical" to make it vertical. NOTE: In Vertical mode the Divider can't contain texts.
